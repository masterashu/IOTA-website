Creating the Imaginary

IOTA is the Technical Club of Indian Institute of Information Technology, Sri City.
IOTA provides students with opportunities to gain experience from real life projects given by various companies working in AI/ML, AR/VR, Software Development, IOT and Cybersecurity.

Our Domains

Artificial Intelligence

We are using AI and ML techniques to create new solution for variour common problems. Our students work in vast areas like Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing, and Meta Learning.

Internet Of Things

IOT provides a wide range of options to our traditional devices. Smart cars and Smart homes are taking over and our students are ready to lead. IOT can be used to automate many tasks to make lives easier and better.

Cybersecurity

With the advent of internet age security and privacy have become a great concern for people. To ensure that we are shielded from cyber attacks, our students keep up with the security standards. 

Software Development

We have experienced students working in many fields of software development such as Web development, Mobile (Android/iOS) development and Desktop application development.
